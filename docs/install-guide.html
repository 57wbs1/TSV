Quick FAQ

Why install? Isn't opening the link enough?
Installing runs the app full-screen (no browser UI), launches faster, and works offline — your status updates will queue and sync when you're back online.
My PIN is 0000 by default
Change it after your first login via Settings → Account → Change PIN.
Location permission — why does it ask?
Only used when you tap "Leaving Hotel" so your buddies and the duty officer can see you on the map. You can deny it and still use the app; location just won't auto-capture.
Telegram notifications?
The bot sends automated reminders (0600H weather, 1900H preview, 2300H SITREP, 0200H curfew). You'll receive them in your syndicate's Telegram group — no setup needed.
