§ 1Install it like a native app

Open the link on your phone and add it to your home screen. Works on iPhone and Android. Once installed it runs full-screen, caches everything for patchy Wi-Fi, and remembers your session.

iPhone · Safari

  1. Open https://57wbs1.github.io/TSV/ in Safari. Chrome on iOS will not let you install it — only Safari can.
  2. Tap the Share icon 􀈂 at the bottom.
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.
  4. Open TSV BKK from your home screen.

Android · Chrome

  1. Open https://57wbs1.github.io/TSV/ in Chrome (or any Chromium browser — Edge, Samsung Internet, etc.).
  2. Chrome usually shows a banner at the bottom: "Add TSV BKK to Home screen". Tap it. If the banner doesn't appear, go to the next step.
  3. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right), then Install app (or Add to Home screen on older Chrome). Confirm with Install.
  4. Open TSV BKK from your app drawer or home screen.

First run · both platforms

Sign in with the PIN your admin gave you. On first login you'll be prompted to set your own — pick something memorable, because the admin can reset it but won't see what you choose.

Why bother installing

The installed PWA gets its own icon, runs full-screen without the browser URL bar eating space, caches everything for patchy airport and hotel Wi-Fi, and survives restarts without asking you to sign in again.

§ 2Your daily rhythm

Five things to touch each day. Most take under 30 seconds.

  • 0800H Mark parade stateIf you're anything other than Present (fever, out for the day, etc.), open Tracker → Parade and update your status.
  • 0830H Parade state goes to commandYour syndicate IC and the leadership chat see the parade-state roll. Your job is to have yourself correctly marked by 0800H — after that, the message goes out.
  • During Leave / return flowsAny time you leave the hotel, hit Leaving → pick buddies → state a location. Hit Return to Hotel when you're back.
  • 0200H CurfewBe marked back in by 0200H BKK. Tap Return to Hotel on your way into the lobby so your status flips before the midnight SITREP fires.
  • 0600H Weather dropsDaily weather + programme preview lands in Telegram. Read it before breakfast.
Hard cut-off

Self-update parade state by 0800H and be accounted for back in hotel by 0200H. Missing either creates real admin workload for the Duty Officer.

§ 4Parade state — update yourself by 0800H

Everyone defaults to Present. If that's not true for the day — sick, hospitalised, absent from trip, whatever — you must update it before the 0830H roll to command.

How to update ›
  1. Open Tracker tab → Parade sub-tab.
  2. Find your row (your syndicate is shown by default).
  3. Tap ✏️ Edit.
  4. Type your status — examples: Sick in Hotel 26 Apr, Hospitalised, Absent from Trip UFN.
  5. The screen saves immediately. Your syndicate IC sees it live.
Send an ad-hoc parade state ›

If statuses change mid-day (officer back from hospital, scheduled return, etc.), anyone can re-send a fresh parade state to command.

  1. Tracker → Parade → 📤 Send Parade State.
  2. Pick which syndicates to include (or leave all blank for mass-send).
  3. Add optional remarks — e.g. All personnel accounted for.
  4. Send. The message goes to the leadership chat.

§ 5Leaving the hotel — buddy system

Every movement out of the hotel must be logged. This is how Command knows who's together, where, and how to reach you if things go sideways.

  1. From Home, tap 🚶 Leaving Hotel.
  2. Pick your syndicate, then tap the officers you're going out with (buddy-system).
  3. Type a location at the top — e.g. Asok market, Terminal 21 — dinner.
  4. Optionally tap Share GPS if you want your location visible on the Map tab to admins (useful if there's a risk of getting separated).
  5. Hit confirm. You're logged as OUT. Your buddies are flipped to OUT automatically with your location attached.
Buddy tips

Only tap buddies who are actually walking out the door with you. If someone in your group heads back early, they should hit Return to Hotel themselves — don't do it on their behalf.

§ 6Returning to hotel — by 0200H

When you walk through the hotel lobby, tap 🏨 Return to Hotel on the Home tab. That flips your status back to IN and stops GPS sharing.

If you don't

The 0200H curfew SITREP will list you as OUT. Expect a phone call from the Duty Officer.

§ 7Calendar & transport

Open the Calendar tab for the daily programme — visits, timings, attire, locations. Tap any event to expand details.

Transport sub-tab

Shows your bus or flight allocation. Times, gates, drivers, remarks.

Boarding — Syndicate IC duty SYN IC›

When your syndicate is due to board:

  1. Open Calendar → Transport.
  2. Tap your bus or flight.
  3. Tap Board for each syndicate as they step onto the vehicle — or Save Progress to record partial boarding.
  4. Once everyone's aboard, tap Send SITREP. A boarding-complete message drops in Telegram.
  5. When the vehicle pushes off, tap Pushing — optionally add remarks. Another Telegram update fires.
  6. On arrival, tap Dropped to free the vehicle for the next cycle.

§ 8Ad-hoc SITREP

Scheduled SITREPs fire at 2300H and 0200H. If movement changes during the day — buses divert, half the syn splits off — fire an ad-hoc one.

Primary: SYN IC

SYN ICs get a 📤 Send SITREP button on the Home tab. One tap, pick syndicates to include, optional remarks, done.

Fallback: anyone

If your SYN IC is unreachable, everyone can send an ad-hoc SITREP from Tracker → list view → 📤 Send SITREP. Same outcome.

§ 9Incident reports — SOP tab

Anything that's not routine — medical, security, missing person, diversion — gets logged as an IR. SYN IC only creates them, but the message trail is public to Command.

Create a new IR SYN IC›
  1. Open SOP tab → + New Incident Report.
  2. Fill Nature (med / security / other), Description, When/Where, Course & Syn involved, NOK informed (Y/N/N/A).
  3. Tick Send to Telegram.
  4. Submit. A 🚨 IR NEW message lands in Command chat with an ID like IR03.
Post an update SYN IC›

As the incident unfolds, keep the feed current. Every update re-sends the full history to Telegram so Command has one canonical thread.

  1. Open the IR from the SOP tab.
  2. Tap ➕ Add Update.
  3. Write what changed — e.g. Officer admitted to BNH Hospital, vitals stable.
  4. Send. 📝 IR UPDATE #n drops in chat with the original summary + all prior updates attached.
Close the incident SYN IC›

When resolved, tick Close Out on the update form. A ✅ IR CLOSED message fires. The IR stays viewable for the rest of the trip but no further updates can be added.

§ 10Map & Rooms Tracker

Map

Shows SCOPE visit pins, bus locations, and live GPS for anyone who opted to share. Tap any pin for details. Useful before you step out — know where your syn already is.

Rooms Tracker

Update your hotel room number once. Your SYN IC needs it to physically find you in an emergency.

  1. Open Tracker → Rooms.
  2. Tap your name, enter your room number.
  3. Save. Done for the trip.

§ 11SOP — contingencies

The SOP tab holds every contingency the TSV committee pre-wrote: medical escalation, lost passport, bus breakdown, demonstration avoidance, hotel lockout. Read through it once on the flight in. Know what's there so you can find it under pressure.
