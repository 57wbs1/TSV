FAQ

Safari won't let me install it.Check you're opening the link in Safari, not Chrome. Only Safari can install PWAs on iOS. Open the page, tap Share, scroll to Add to Home Screen.
App shows a spinning circle and never loads.Force-quit Safari or the TSV app. Reopen. If it still hangs, kill the app and reopen once more — the service worker recovers on its second boot.
Why isn't my SITREP or IR button visible on Home?Those surface only for SYN ICs. If you think you should be an IC, message the admin via Settings → Report an Issue.
Can I leave the hotel without tapping Leaving?Technically yes, but your status stays IN while you're actually OUT, which breaks the roll. Always log it — 15 seconds saves command 15 minutes.
Is GPS required when I leave?No. It's optional. A text location is enough. GPS only adds a pin on the Map tab.
I forgot my PIN.Ask the admin to reset it. They can issue a fresh default, then you change it from Settings on first login.
I marked my buddy OUT by accident.They can open the app and tap Return to Hotel if they're still in the hotel. Or file a correction with your IC.
I can't see my syndicate members in Parade.Scroll. The default view shows your syn. If your syn is selected at the top but the list is empty, pull-to-refresh on the list.
My reflection submission showed an error.Your text is still in the form — the fields do not clear on failure. Check your connection, tap Submit again. If it persists, flag via Settings.
Telegram message didn't arrive after I submitted.Open the IR or the SITREP event. SYN IC ICs have a 📤 Resend button on each IR event. For SITREPs, just fire another ad-hoc from Home or Tracker.
The app looks broken after a deploy.Force-reload: pull down on the main screen or close-reopen the app. The service worker auto-updates and the next open should show the new version.
I'm in a bad network zone and taps don't seem to save.The app queues writes and retries when you're back online. Wait for signal, open the app, give it a few seconds. If the queue indicator is red, pull to refresh.

Three rules

  1. Mark parade state by 0800H if not Present.
  2. Log every hotel exit and tap Return when you come back.
  3. Flag problems early — to your SYN IC, or via Settings → Report.
